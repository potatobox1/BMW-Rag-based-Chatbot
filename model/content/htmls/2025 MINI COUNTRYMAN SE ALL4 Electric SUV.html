Countryman SE ALL4 FAQs.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is there an all-electric MINI Countryman?

Yes, there is an all-electric MINI Countryman SE ALL4 set for production in July 2024. It’s a multi-functional small electric SUV with a Smokey Green exterior body color plus a Vibrant Silver roof and mirror caps. The 2025 MINI Countryman SE ALL4 is the first of MINI’s Next Gen models announced.

What are the new features on the all-electric MINI Countryman SE ALL4?

New features on the all-electric MINI Countryman SE ALL4 include a 9.4” round OLED display with a refreshing user interface design, MINI Operating System 9 optimized for touch control and developed in-house by the BMW Group, MINI’s Intelligent Personal Assistant, and eight MINI Experience Modes which use light projections to set the stage for an immersive driving experience.

The exterior features 20” Windmill Spoke 2-tone wheels in Jet Black and Vibrant Silver, along with a prominent C-pillar design element and power dome on the front hood. The interior features a Vescin Vintage Brown colorway and surfaces knitted with a unique 2D technique.

When will the all-electric MINI Countryman SE ALL4 be available?

The all-electric MINI Countryman SE ALL4 will be available to the public in Fall 2024.
