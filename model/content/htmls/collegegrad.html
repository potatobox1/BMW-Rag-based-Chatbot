SAVE $500 ON A NEW OR CERTIFIED PRE-OWNED MINI WITH THE MINI COLLEGE GRAD PROGRAM

Available exclusively through MINI Financial Services. Applicants must finance through MINI Financial Services and meet all requirements of the program to qualify for the incentive. To qualify, applicants must have graduated from an accredited college or university with a graduate, undergraduate or associate's degree earned within the previous 24 months, or be eligible for graduation within the next six months with a verifiable offer of employment. Subject to credit approval. CPO rate based on credit scores. Offer may not be combined with certain other offers. New 2022 MINI models (Excluding electric MINI Cooper SE, Plug-In Hybrid Countryman, and Oxford Edition models), new 2023 MINI models (Excluding electric MINI Cooper SE, Plug-In Hybrid Countryman, and Classic trim models), and all MINI Certified Pre-Owned model years are included in this program. Program is also applicable to lease contract under the same terms and conditions. Program subject to change without notice and is not transferable to other family members or friends outside the household. All lease and financing options are based upon dealer and program requirements and are subject to credit review and approval by MINI Financial Services, a division of BMW Financial Services NA, LLC.

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

  • You've earned an undergraduate, graduate or associate's degree from an accredited college or university within the previous 24 months OR you are eligible to graduate within the next 6 months and have a verifiable offer of employment.
  • Proof of income or employer letter with the Human Resources representative's contact information, stating position/title, and income.
  • You have at least 6 months of credit history with no previous derogatory credit.
  • You have a debt-to-income ratio of less than 45%.
  • You have an automotive payment to income ratio of less than 20%.
  • You can provide a copy of your diploma or your official transcript.
  • Security deposit will be waived unless stipulated by credit.
  • This offer is subject to credit review and approval through MINI Financial Services.
