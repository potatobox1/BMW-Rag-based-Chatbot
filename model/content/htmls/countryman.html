Technology

FUN MEETS
FUNCTIONAL.

MINI Operating System 9 is a software powerhouse optimized for touch and voice control featuring cloud-based navigation and effortless connectivity. Plus, an available copilot is at the ready for direction tips and tricks - just say “Hey Spike” to welcome our first fully-fledged voice assistant into the cockpit.

Technology FEATURES

Make your commute a concert

Standard wireless Apple CarPlay© and Android Auto™ allow you to seamlessly connect your media for endless entertainment and important info.

Never miss a beat

Head-up Display presents key driving information, including navigation, speed, and alerts, while allowing you to keep your eyes on the road.

Get personal

Get more out of your MINI. The MINI App has a fleet of features like Remote Engine Start and the ability to customize your dashboard with a personal photo.

Experience modes

A MODE FOR
EVERY MOMENT.

Vivid Mode

Have your MINI match the music, with display colors and light projections sourced from the cover artwork of your media using automatic color selection technology.

Core Mode

Core Mode in the new MINI color shade Laguna is sophisticated and modern. The projection and ambient lighting are automatically matched to this color.

Green Mode

With a focus on the essential details, this green-hued mode is designed to bring you back down to Earth and highlights the more sustainable side to MINI mobility.

Timeless Mode

With its Serif font and large dial, this mode recalls the heritage of the MINI brand and presents it with a modern Trim against a bright background. A dark setting also makes Timeless Mode perfect for night.

Go-Kart Mode

Go-Kart Mode is all about amping up the fun. Stiffer steering feel and increased accelerator pedal sensitivity is guaranteed to bring you more smiles per mile. 

Balance Mode

The animations and colors are kept deliberately understated in this simplistic and calming blue-hued mode designed for ultimate peace of mind.

Trail Mode

Trail Mode taps into a love for exploration with natural-inspired lighting and a visual reference to the compass, symbolizing the joy of discovering new paths.

Personal Mode

Personal Mode is all about you and what you love. It offers the ability to customize the background of your display with up to three personal pictures. Personal Mode will become available in July via Remote Software Upgrade.

Safety

PROTECTION 
PACKED.

Safety features

Standard Active Blind Spot Detection

Visual cues in exterior mirrors alert you to hazards in blind spots, with steering wheel vibration.

Standard Lane Departure Warning

A multi-camera array helps keep the vehicle in its lane and alerts the driver to unintentional drift.

Standard Front Collision Warning with Brake Intervention

This system alerts drivers to collision threats, and automatically activates braking if an immediate intervention is needed.

Available Active Driving Assistant Professional

Innovative camera and radar-based driver assistance systems provide partly-automated support to deliver peace of mind.

COUNTRYMAN S ALL4 FAQs

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does the 2025 MINI Countryman S ALL4 cost?

The price of a 2025 MINI Countryman S ALL4 starts at $38,900, and $46,900 for the JCW Countryman ALL4, and $45,200 for the Countryman SE ALL4.

How much horsepower does the 2025 Countryman S ALL4 have?

The 2025 Countryman S ALL4 delivers 241 horsepower, in addition to 295 lb-ft of torque. It accelerates from 0-60 mph in 6.2 seconds and reaches top speeds of 130 [150] mph.  

Which transmission is the 2025 Countryman S ALL4 available in?

The 2025 Countryman S ALL4 comes with a 7-Speed Automatic Dual Clutch Transmission.

How many miles per gallon does the MINI Countryman S ALL4 get?

The 2025 Countryman S ALL4 gets 27 combined mpg, 24 mpg in the city and 32 mpg on the highway.  

What is the 2025 MINI Countryman S ALL4’s towing capacity?

The 2025 Countryman S ALL4 has a towing capacity of an estimated 3,500 pounds with the available factory-installed trailer hitch.
