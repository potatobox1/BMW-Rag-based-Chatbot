What’s included

FEATURE FORWARD

It’s not just the extra doors that come standard. Enjoy a Panoramic Moonroof, Parking Assistant, Wireless Device Charging, Heated Front Seats, a Heated Steering Wheel, LED Headlights, and so much more at no additional cost.

EFFORTLESS ENTRY

The days of digging through pockets and purses to find keys are long gone with seamless entry via Comfort Access and keyless ways to lock and unlock your MINI via MINI Digital Key Plus on the MINI App.¹

NEVER MISS A BEAT

Key driving information, including speed, directions, and alerts, are all right before your eyes with MINI Head-up Display. And with the MINI Toggle Bar and industry-first 9.4” OLED Display taking center stage, the rest is at your fingertips.

Comfort Cruise

Sit back in Sport Seats made of cloth and Vescin, a leather-free upholstery made of recycled materials that’s as soft as it is durable, and kick back with 1.5 more inches of rear legroom than the Cooper 2 Door.

Technology

A TECH 
RENAISSANCE.

MINI Operating System 9 is a software powerhouse optimized for touch and voice control featuring cloud-based navigation, effortless connectivity, and a copilot at the ready for direction tips and tricks. Just say “Hey MINI” to welcome our first fully-fledged voice assistant, MINI Intelligent Personal Assistant, into the cockpit.

Technology FEATURES

Fun meets functional

From the ability to pre-condition your MINI and customize your display with a personal photo to available features like Anti-Theft Recorder and Remote Parking, there’s more to your MINI just a tap away on the MINI App.

Play while parked

A gaming console integrated directly into the MINI OLED Display, Available AirConsole, offers endless entertainment options. With up to 50 third party apps to choose from, you’ll never have a boring rest stop again.

No more missed exits

With available MINI Navigation AR, turning left or right has never been more straightforward. Directional cues transposed onto visual renderings of the driving route via augmented reality make driving a walk in the park.

Experience modes

A MODE FOR
EVERY MOMENT.

Personal Mode

Personal Mode is all about you and what you love. It offers the ability to customize the background of your display with up to three personal pictures. Personal Mode will become available in July via Remote Software Update.

Core Mode

Core Mode in the new MINI color Laguna is sophisticated and modern. The projections and ambient lighting are automatically matched to this relaxing shade.

Timeless Mode 

With its Serif font and large dial, this mode recalls the heritage of the MINI brand and presents it with a modern Trim against a bright background. A dark setting also makes Timeless Mode perfect for night.

Go-Kart Mode

Go-Kart Mode is all about amping up the fun. Stiffer steering feel and increased accelerator pedal sensitivity is guaranteed to bring you more smiles per mile.

Vivid Mode

Have your MINI match the music with display colors and light projections sourced from the cover artwork of your media using automatic color selection technology.

Green Mode

With a focus on the essential details, this green-hued mode is designed to bring you back down to Earth and highlights the more sustainable side to MINI mobility.

Balance Mode

The animations and colors are kept deliberately understated in this simplistic and calming blue-hued mode designed for ultimate peace of mind.

SUPERSIZED

Safety features

Available Active Blind Spot Detection

Visual cues in exterior mirrors alert the driver to hazards in blind spots, with steering wheel vibration and collision avoidance systems activating when changing lanes.

Available Lane Departure Warning

A multi-camera array helps keep the vehicle centered in its lane and alerts the driver to any unintentional drift.

Available Front Collision Warning with Brake Intervention

This system alerts the driver to potential collision threats, and automatically activates the brakes if immediate intervention is needed.

Available Rear Cross Traffic Warning and Rear Collision Prevention

Rear Collision Prevention provides visual and audible alerts to help the driver avoid potential collisions when reversing.

Available Exit Warning

Exit Warning uses visual and acoustic alerts to notify the driver when other vehicles or cyclists are approaching from behind as the vehicle doors are opened.

Available Active Cruise Control with Stop and Go Function

Innovative camera and radar-based assistance systems provide a more relaxed driving experience for long journeys by maintaining a set distance from the vehicle in front. In stop-and-go traffic, the system supports the driver by automatically slowing the vehicle to a stop and accelerating when the traffic situation permits.

COOPER S 4 DOOR FAQs

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much is the 2025 MINI Cooper S 4 Door?

The all-new 2025 MINI Cooper S 4 Door has a starting MSRP of $33,200. Starting MSRP excludes the destination and handling fee of $995.

When will the 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door models be available?

The 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door will be available in Fall 2024 and the MINI Cooper S 4 Door is available now.

How do I build and price the 2025 MINI Cooper 4 models?

You can build and price your 2025 MINI Cooper S 4 Door on the Build Yours page.

What are the new features on the 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door models?

New features on the 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door models include a 9.4” circular OLED display, Remote Engine Start, MINI’s Intelligent Personal Assistant, leather-free Sport Seats, MINI Head-up Display, and more.

How fast does the 2025 MINI Cooper S 4 Door go from 0-60 mph?

The 2025 MINI Cooper S 4 Door can accelerate from 0-60 mph in 6.5 seconds. In addition, it delivers 201 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque.  

What are the dimensions of the 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door models and how do they compare to the Cooper 2 Door?

The 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door models are 158.9 inches long (+6.3 inches more than the 2 Door), 68.7 inches wide and just over 57.6 inches tall (+1.2 inches more than the 2 Door). In addition, the MINI Cooper 4 Door models come with a larger wheelbase.

Are the 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door models available in all-wheel drive?

The 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door models are not available in all-wheel drive. However, the 2025 Countryman S ALL4, 2025 Countryman SE ALL4, and 2025 JCW Countryman ALL4 come with intelligent all-wheel drive.

What transmissions are available with the 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door?

The 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door models come with Standard 7-Speed Automatic Dual Clutch Transmission and are no longer available with a manual transmission.

What colors are the 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door models available in?

The 2025 MINI Cooper 4 Door models are available in Sunny Side Yellow, Chili Red II, British Racing Green IV metallic, Midnight Black II, Nanuq White metallic, Melting Silver III metallic, Indigo Sunset Blue, Blazing Blue, Icy Sunshine Blue, and Ocean Wave Green.

¹ MINI Digital Key Plus is compatible with: Apple devices, Samsung devices (running Android 13.1 or later) including Galaxy S23+, S23 Ultra, S22+, S22 Ultra, S21+, S21 Ultra, Z Fold4, Z Fold3, Note20 Ultra (in markets with Samsung Wallet), and Google devices (running Android 13.1 or later) including Pixel 7 Pro and Pixel 6 Pro.
