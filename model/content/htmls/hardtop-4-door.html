EXTERIOR

The bigger the better.

Friends come standard in the 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door. Bring your friends. Bring the kids. Bring the dog. Bring it on. With double the doors and more room for everyone, you can have your fill of fun in this compact hatchback. It’s everything you love about MINI, with more where that came from.

BUILD YOURS

Loads of exterior features to discover.

Distinct design.

Instantly recognizable, yet remarkably subtle. The Hardtop 4 Door is adorned with a sleek Piano Black exterior, from the door handles and side scuttle to the headlight and taillight rings.

Vibrant visibility.

Be cool and confident in your course of action. The Hardtop 4 Door has standard LED Headlights and the classic Union Jack taillights to guide the way, pairing nicely with the front and back grilles.

Signature swagger.

Top off the ride with a splash of color. The Hardtop 4 Door has a multitone roof—available in Soul Blue, Alpine White, and Chili Red gradients—that’s plastered with exclusive painting technology.

Fine finishes.

Look sharp and smooth as you hit the streets. The Hardtop 4 Door is flashier than flashy with hot wheels and crisp air vents, along with air curtains for a more aerodynamic drive.

INTERIOR

One size fits all.

With more headroom and legroom for your group, plus plenty of cargo room for all your stuff, the 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door hatchback is the perfect example of how you can dial up comfort without compromising on anything else.

Cooper S variant shown.

Amped up interior design and tech.

Steering Wheel

The Hardtop 4 Door has a multifunctional sport leather steering wheel that’s gamified with Lane Departure Warning, which notifies drivers about lane drifting with rhythmic vibrations.

Cargo Space

The Hardtop 4 Door has the space for all kinds of adventures with over 40 cubic feet of cargo volume and over 13 cubic feet of trunk volume to pack belongings.

Dashboard

The Hardtop 4 Door is graced with a glorious dashboard composition fusing an 8.8” touchscreen as the centerpiece with a Dynamic Digital Instrument Cluster for easier view of information.

Seating

The Hardtop 4 Door fits five adults comfortably, averaging 36.9 inches of legroom between the front and back rows in both variants to merge with almost 40 inches of headroom.

Pack some snacks and pick up your friends. It’s time to make big MINI memories in the 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door, which comes fully equipped with a TwinPower Turbo engine configuration that will get you from 0-60 mph in as little as 6.6 seconds with the Cooper S variant.

Hardtop 4 Door FAQs.

COMMON QUESTIONS ABOUT THE MINI COUNTRYMAN

How much does a 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door cost?

The cost of a 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door is $26,800 for the Starting MSRP of a Cooper engine variant and $30,100 for the Starting MSRP of a Cooper S engine variant.

What are the dimensions of a 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door?

The dimensions of a 2024 Hardtop 4 Door measure an average of 158.8 inches long, 75.9 inches wide (including mirrors), and just over 56 inches tall with a 101.1 inch wheelbase across both engine variants. The Hardtop 4 Door is bigger than a Hardtop 2 Door in length and height, with a larger wheelbase too.

What is the weight of the 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door?

The curb weight of a MINI Hardtop 4 Door, or its weight including a full tank of fuel and all standard equipment, is 2,892 lbs for the Cooper engine variant and 2,963 lbs for the Cooper S engine variant.

How much horsepower does a 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door have?

A MINI Hardtop 4 Door has 134 horsepower for the Cooper engine variant and 189 horsepower for the Cooper S engine variant, with 7.9-second and 6.6-second 0-60 mph times for each, respectively. The top speed for the MINI Cooper Hardtop 4 Door is 129 mph, while the top speed for the MINI Cooper S Hardtop 4 Door is 145 mph.

Is the 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door available with all-wheel drive?

The MINI Hardtop 4 Door is not available with all-wheel drive. However, our intelligent ALL4 all-wheel drive system is available as an option with various Clubman and Countryman model engine variants.

Is the 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door available in a manual transmission?

The MINI Hardtop 4 Door compact hatchback is available in six-speed manual or automatic transmissions.

How many miles per gallon (mpg) does the 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door get?

The 2024 MINI Hardtop 4 Door gets an estimated combined city/highway fuel economy of 32 mpg for the Cooper engine variant and 31 mpg for the Cooper S engine variant.
