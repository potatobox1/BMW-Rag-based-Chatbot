Discover the car that’s as unique as you are. The car that drives different. The car that can take a wrong turn and turn it into the right move. Discover the 2024 MINI Hardtop 2 Door hatchback. There’s nothing quite like it. And that’s why you’ll love it.

Loads of exterior features to discover.

Dapper design.

Stand out as you take corners like a champ. The Hardtop 2 Door is furnished with auto-leveling LED headlights and a patterned front grille to go along with optional Piano Black exterior.

Fine finishes.

Make a fashion statement. The Hardtop 2 Door stretches about 152 inches long from the LED headlights all the way to the bumper, tailpipe finisher, and striped Union Jack taillights.

Fresh functionality.

Point A to Point B is safe and stylish. The Hardtop 2 Door hatchback has a secure Anti-lock Brake System (ABS) and Multitone Roof available in three exclusive color gradients.

Delightful drive.

Be bold and breathtaking. The Hardtop 2 Door comes with multiple wheel variations to pair with details like the model badge and side scuttle plus the front and rear “wings”.

zone.

The subcompact hatchback you’ve always loved is more magical than ever before. From amped-up ambient lighting and a reinvigorated dashboard to dandy designs for the steering wheel and air vents, you’ll fall head over wheels for the funnest car on the road.

Cooper S variant shown.

Loads of interior features to discover.

Steering Wheel

The Hardtop 2 Door has a multifunctional sport leather steering wheel that’s gamified with Lane Departure Warning, which notifies drivers about lane drifting with rhythmic vibrations.

Dynamic Digital Instrument Cluster

The Hardtop 2 Door replaces the Analog Cluster with a 5.5” Dynamic Digital Instrument Cluster for easier view of information like vehicle speed, driving modes, and charging level if applicable.

Seating

The Hardtop 2 Door comfortably seats up to four adults, with an average of 36.1 inches of legroom between the front and back rows across all of its engine variants.

Dashboard

The Hardtop 2 Door includes a beautiful dashboard composition featuring a large 8.8” touchscreen in the center instrument display with an intuitive interface and High Gloss black detail.

Extras

The Hardtop 2 Door is graced with a built-in Driver Assistant System plus configurable ambient lighting, heated front seats, and optional Rear Park Distance Control.

Hardtop 2 Door FAQs.

COMMON QUESTIONS ABOUT THE MINI HARDTOP 2 DOOR

What is a MINI Cooper Hardtop?

The Hardtop can be considered the classic MINI, available in both two- and four-door models. It can be completely customizable and always provides our signature fun, go-kart drive.

How much does a MINI Hardtop 2 Door cost?

The price of a 2024 MINI Hardtop 2 Door starts at $25,800 for the Cooper engine variant, $29,100 for the Cooper S engine variant, $30,900 for the Cooper SE Electric engine variant, and $35,400 for the John Cooper Works engine variant.

What are the dimensions of a MINI Hardtop 2 Door?

The 2024 MINI Hardtop 2 Door measures an average of 152.4 inches long, 75.9 inches wide (including mirrors), and about 56 inches tall with a 98.2-inch wheelbase across all engine variants.

What is the weight of the MINI Hardtop 2 Door?

The MINI Hardtop 2 Door weighs 2,769 lbs for the Cooper engine variant, 2,841 lbs for the Cooper S engine variant, 2,951 lbs for the JCW engine variant, and 3,143 lbs for the Cooper SE engine variant.

How fast is a MINI Hardtop 2 Door?

The 2024 MINI Hardtop 2 Door has an estimated top speed of 153 miles per hour for the John Cooper Works engine variant while reaching 0-60 mph in approximately 5.9 seconds. The Cooper and Cooper S engine variants have estimated top speeds of 130 mph and 146 mph, respectively, while reaching 0-60 mph in 7.6 seconds for the Cooper and 6.4 seconds for the Cooper S. The Cooper SE engine variant has an estimated top speed of 93 miles per hour while reaching 0-60 mph in approximately 6.9 seconds.

Is the MINI Hardtop 2 Door available with all-wheel drive?

No, the MINI Hardtop 2 Door model is not outfitted with our intelligent ALL4 all-wheel drive system. However, ALL4 all-wheel drive capabilities are available as an option with various Clubman and Countryman model engine variants.

Is the MINI Hardtop 2 Door available in a manual transmission?

Yes, the MINI Hardtop 2 Door subcompact hatchback is available in six-speed manual or automatic transmissions.

Is the MINI Hardtop 2 Door a Convertible?

The MINI Hardtop comes in both two- and four-door body styles. MINI also has a convertible option known as the MINI Convertible.

How many seats does the MINI Hardtop 2 Door have?

The 2024 MINI Hardtop 2 Door seats four passengers comfortably across all four engine variants.
