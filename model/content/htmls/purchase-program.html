Select Financing

MINI Select balloon financing is an affordable financing option that offers all the perks of ownership, with lower monthly payments compared to traditional financing.    

At the end of your contract, your balloon balance is due. You can pay the balloon balance in full, apply to refinance the balance, or, if you have equity in your MINI, you may be able to use the trade-in value to help cover your balance.


MINI SELECT FINANCING HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Available in most states

  • Eligible on new, Certified Pre-Owned, or pre-owned MINI vehicles

  • No mileage restrictions or wear and use charges

MINI OwnersChoice

A balloon financing option is available to drivers whose vehicle is garaged in Illinois, Texas, and Georgia. It offers low monthly payments and flexibility at the end of the term.

This option allows you to pay the balloon balance in full or apply to refinance the balance at the end of the term. You may also pay off the vehicle in full.
