U.S. MINI DIPLOMATIC SALES PROGRAM FAQ.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE TO PARTICIPATE IN THE U.S. DIPLOMATIC SALES PROGRAM?

Diplomats with the following visa classifications are eligible to participate:

Foreign Diplomats qualify if their VISA classification is:

A1, A2 • G1, G2, G3, G4 • NATO 1, NATO 2, NATO 3, NATO 4 • Note: E1 Visas do not qualify

U.S. Diplomats qualify if:

Their passport number begins with “9”. • Their passport number begins with “8”, and is accompanied by a letter on official letterhead verifying their employment.

Members of International Organizations:

Must possess a Laissez-passer service pass issued by the United Nations. • A letter from the mission or proof of assignment from the International Organization must accompany the request

WHAT DOCUMENTATION IS REQUIRED TO PARTICIPATE IN THE PROGRAM?

In order to participate in the U.S. MINI Diplomatic Sales program, you will need to present the following documents to your local MINI Dealer: • Visa • Passport / Laissez Passer • Letter of Employment Verification (if applicable) Your Visa, Passport, or Laissez Passer must be valid for a minimum of 6 months after retail delivery.

WHAT IS THE SAVINGS AMOUNT?

The savings is 5% off the base MSRP.

U.S. MINI Diplomatic Sales Program excludes base Cooper models and is not valid in combination with any MINI lease. Talk to your local MINI dealer about next steps for making a purchase through this program.

HOW DO I PLACE MY ORDER?

To begin the order process, please contact your local MINI Dealer.

CAN I ORDER A NON – U.S. SPECIFICATION VEHICLES THROUGH THE U.S. MINI DIPLOMATIC SALES PROGRAM?

No, this program is only available for U.S. specification vehicles delivered in the United States. Please contact your local MINI Dealer to receive the latest list of eligible models for this program.
