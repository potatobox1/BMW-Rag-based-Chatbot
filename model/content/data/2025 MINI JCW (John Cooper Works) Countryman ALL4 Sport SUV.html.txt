Visual cues in exterior mirrors alert you to hazards in blind spots, with steering wheel vibration.

JCW COUNTRYMAN ALL4 FAQs

How much does the 2025 MINI JCW Countryman ALL4 cost?

The price of a 2025 MINI JCW Countryman ALL4 starts at $46,900, and $38,900 for the Countryman S ALL4, and $45,200 for the Countryman SE ALL4.

How much horsepower does the 2025 Countryman ALL4 JCW have?

The 2025 JCW Countryman ALL4 delivers 312 horsepower, in addition to 295 lb-ft of torque. It accelerates from 0-60 mph in 5.2 seconds and reaches top speeds of 130 [155] mph.

Which transmission is the 2025 JCW Countryman ALL4 available in?

The 2025 JCW Countryman ALL4 comes with a 7-Speed Sport Automatic Dual Clutch Transmission.

How many miles per gallon does the MINI JCW Countryman ALL4 get?

The 2025 MINI JCW Countryman ALL4 gets 26 combined mpg, 23 mpg in the city and 30 mpg on the highway
