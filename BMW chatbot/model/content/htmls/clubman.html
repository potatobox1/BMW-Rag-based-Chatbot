SOPHISTICATION AT ITS FUNNEST

With its luscious exterior design and roomy interior crafted from the finest materials, the MINI Clubman is the most sophisticated small station wagon we've ever made. If that's not enough, its split-rear doors and optional ALL4 all-wheel drive put it in a class of its own.

REFINED YET THRILLING

Bold sophistication clashes with the thrills of the MINI drive through contemporary design details that marry our heritage of quality with ingenuity. Available as a Cooper S, with optional ALL4 all-wheel drive, or a John Cooper Works ALL4. The MINI Clubman has plenty of power and will leave you with a smile on your face.

PREMIUM, NOT
PRETENTIOUS

The MINI Clubman is a small station wagon offering incredible customization options from paint, roof and mirror color combinations to premium interior trims and upholstery choices. The Clubman offers comfortable seating for five, a Dual-Pane Panoramic Moonroof, and spacious rear boot with split-rear doors for easy cargo loading.

HIGH-END
TECHNOLOGY

Rest assured, the MINI Clubman’s technology is there for your every need. Enjoy driver-centric onboard displays, intelligent systems, and even more ways to connect all your devices. Inside you’ll have access to the 8.8” Touchscreen center display, Apple CarPlay®, intelligent cruise control, navigation with real-time traffic, and more.

EXCLUSIVE
ROOF AESTHETICS

An absolute highlight to our 2024 MINI Clubman is our Multitone Roof, available in three different Soul Blue, Alpine White, and Chili Red color gradients. It’s exclusive painting technology with the swagger that sets MINI apart.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What model variants are available for the MINI Clubman?

The MINI Clubman station wagon is available in three model variants in the US. The Cooper S is a front-wheel drive model with a TwinPower Turbo engine that has 189 horsepower, also available with optional ALL4 all-wheel drive. The John Cooper Works ALL4 is our sportiest model available, also with a TwinPower Turbo engine and 301 horsepower that rockets you from 0-60 in 4.6 seconds.

Is the MINI Clubman available with all-wheel drive?

Yes, the MINI Clubman has two options equipped with ALL4 all-wheel drive: the Cooper S ALL4, starting at $36,150, or the John Cooper Works ALL4 starting at $42,400.

How many seats does the MINI Clubman have?

The MINI Clubman can comfortably seat up to five passengers.
