DESIGNED TO BE CERTIFIED FROM THE START

MINI CPO is a smart decision that comes with a side of adventure. With MINI CPO, you get the added peace of mind that your car continues to meet our standards and expectations with thorough inspections, 24/7 Roadside Assistance, and the comprehensive MINI CPO Limited Warranty.

ELIGIBILITY

SELECTIVE FROM THE START

Although every MINI is young at heart, a Certified Pre-Owned MINI really is. We limit CPO MINIs to be 5 years young (or younger) with less than 60,000 miles driven.

INSPECTION

RIGOROUSLY INSPECTED

MINI trained technicians inspect and certify every MINI CPO candidate with a very detailed checklist to make sure every Certified Pre-Owned MINI holds up to its name.

WARRANTY

BEST IN CLASS PEACE OF MIND

Every MINI Certified vehicle is backed by our MINI Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) Limited Warranty. This provides comprehensive coverage for up to 6-year/unlimited mile warranty from original in-service date, including 24/7 Roadside Assistance plan.

THE MINI CPO CHECKLIST

Less than 5 years young

We put an age limit on all of our CPO MINIs to ensure the best MINI drive.

Less than 60,000 thrilling miles driven

Every CPO MINI will have less 60,000 miles driven.

Up to date CARFAX® or AutoCheck® Report

Our updated CARFAX® or AutoCheck® Report details information like if a MINI was in an accident, how many owners the car has had, and other historical information. You will have full access to these reports when looking to purchase your CPO MINI.

Original, genuine MINI parts

We guarantee that your CPO MINI will be reconditioned with 100% genuine MINI parts. Even if your car has been upgraded, we make sure we maintain the high level MINI standard.

Passes a tire, wheel, and brake inspection

As part of our 50 point wheel assembly inspection, we rigorously check all tires, wheels, and brakes. We look closely at tire tread depth and perform a sidewall, brake pad, and rotor inspection.

Passes a body condition, fit and finish inspection

As part of our 54 point body condition, fit and finish inspection, we rigorously check every inch of the MINI to see if it meets MINI guidelines and standards. We look closely at bumpers, hood, doors, roof, and all other exterior features as well as the glass area, interior, trunk, keys and more.

Passes a mechanical inspection

As part of our 46 point mechanical inspection, we rigorously check all of the mechanics of the MINI to see if it meets MINI guidelines and standards. We look closely at how everything is assembled from headlights to airbags to trunk lock.

Passes a stationary and rolling road test

As part of our 80 point stationary and rolling road test, we test the MINI for at least 20 uninterrupted minutes for at least 5 continuous miles. During these tests, we look closely at the driver’s seat functions, mirror functions, navigation system, windshield, pedal function, vehicle handling and much more.

Approved by our tech, service manager, and CPO manager

In order for any MINI to get the coveted title of Certified Pre Owned, it must get official sign off but a certified MINI Technician, Service Manager, and MINI CPO Manager.

Sirius XM Included

To enhance your MINI drive, we’ll include 3 free months of Sirius XM satellite radio on appropriately equipped vehicles.

THE MINI CPO WARRANTY

The MINI Certified Pre-Owned Limited Warranty provides specific coverage against defects in materials and workmanship. You’re covered for 1 year/unlimited miles after the expiration of the 4-year/50,000-mile MINI New Passenger Car Limited Warranty, for a total of 5 years/unlimited miles. This includes up to 5 years of 24/7 Roadside Assistance.*

Optional Protection

Ensure your MINI is covered if the unexpected comes your way with protection programs that provide extra coverage beyond your warranty.
