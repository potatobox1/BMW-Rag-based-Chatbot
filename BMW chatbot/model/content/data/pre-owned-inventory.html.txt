FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do you determine my trade-in value?

Your trade-in will be independently valued by Kelley Blue Book based on information you provide such as condition and mileage.

Will my local dealership be involved?

Yes, you will be purchasing your MINI directly from the authorized MINI dealer you select during the online purchase process.

Who performs the credit check?

MINI Financial Services, a division of BMW Financial Services NA, LLC will perform the credit check via TransUnion.

Where do I get my car serviced?

You can have your car serviced at your preferred MINI dealer.

What are the requirements for purchasing a car through MINI Anywhere and what is the approval process?

All you’ll need is to be 18 years of age or older, have a permanent U.S. driver’s license, submit a credit application online, and check out online.

How does MINI USA use my information?

MINI USA may collect, use, or share information related to your use of MINI sites or other MINI services. For full information about MINI USA’s use of information, please go to https://www.miniusa.com/footer-navigation/privacy-legal.html.

Do I have to go to the dealer or will my MINI be delivered to me?

Most dealers will be happy to deliver to customers within a manageable radius. You can make delivery or pick-up arrangements directly with your MINI dealer.

Is there a return policy if I don’t like the vehicle?

Once you purchase the car and accept your delivery, you are the owner. We suggest discussing any return related questions directly with your MINI dealer.
